DOTES Experience Capture

Do

What it Represents: The actions, projects, and experiments that provide the foundation for learning.

Purpose: Foster intentionality in activities to capture lessons and insights that inform future efforts.

Key Practices:

  • Identify and document key actions or projects that generated meaningful outcomes.
  • Reflect on what worked, what didn't, and why.
  • Ensure a clear connection between actions and organizational objectives.

Reflect on a specific action or project. What was the goal, and what did you achieve?

Observe

What it Represents: The practice of attentiveness to internal and external dynamics.

Purpose: Develop the ability to identify patterns, trends, and behaviors that shape decision-making.

Key Practices:

  • Record observations about team dynamics, stakeholder interactions, and broader environmental factors.
  • Synthesize insights from observations to uncover new opportunities or challenges.
  • Encourage diverse perspectives to enrich the understanding of what is being observed.

Document what you noticed. What worked? What didn't? What patterns or insights emerged?

Tell

What it Represents: The stories that articulate the organization's experiences, challenges, and growth.

Purpose: Strengthen connection and alignment by sharing narratives that inspire and inform.

Key Practices:

  • Encourage storytelling to capture the depth and nuance of organizational learning.
  • Include voices from across the organization to ensure inclusivity in storytelling.
  • Use stories to reinforce alignment with the mission and inspire stakeholders to act.

Share the story of your experience. Who was involved, and what was the outcome?

Explore

What it Represents: The drive to question, innovate, and test new ideas.

Purpose: Cultivate curiosity and a willingness to explore uncharted territory as a source of resilience.

Key Practices:

  • Create safe spaces for experimentation and ideation.
  • Document outcomes from exploratory efforts, whether successful or not.
  • Use exploration as a tool to adapt to changing conditions and refine strategies.

Identify future opportunities. What new questions or ideas arose from this experience?

Show

What it Represents: The process of demonstrating the value and impact of learning.

Purpose: Reinforce accountability and inspire trust by transparently showcasing progress and achievements.

Key Practices:

  • Share outcomes through visuals, presentations, and reports that resonate with diverse audiences.
  • Highlight milestones and celebrate achievements to foster a culture of growth.
  • Align showcasing efforts with organizational values to ensure authenticity.

Demonstrate the impact. How can you creatively present your insights to inspire others?
