Nonprofit Organizational State Assessment

This assessment will help determine if your organization is in a Start Up, Tune Up, or Level Up state. Answer each question based on your current organizational situation.

Mission Impact & Position

1. Program Model Status: How would you characterize your program model?

2. Ecosystem Position: How would you describe your position in the nonprofit ecosystem?

Operations & Processes

3. Organizational Processes: How developed are your operational processes?

4. Program Delivery Systems: What is the state of your program delivery infrastructure?

Financial Sustainability

5. Funding Status: How would you characterize your funding situation?

6. Resource Allocation: How efficient is your resource allocation?

Team & Culture

7. Organizational Structure: How would you describe your current structure?

8. Human Resources: What is the state of your team?

Community & Stakeholders

9. Stakeholder Relationships: How would you characterize your stakeholder engagement?

10. Impact Opportunity: What is your impact potential?
